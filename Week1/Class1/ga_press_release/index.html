The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies



Recipe by: My Grandma Prep Time

45 Min

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (3 Sticks) butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbl vanilla powder
  • 2 tbl milk
  • 2 tbl vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 baking soda
  • salt
  • chocolate chips
  • walnuts or pecans


How to make Mama's Recipe: The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. Preheat to 350
  2. Beat Butter and Sugar
  3. Stipr Pudding, Milk and Vanilla
  4. Beat in Eggs
  5. Add dry ingredients
  6. Stir in Chips and Nuts
  7. Place dough on cookie sheet
  8. Bake 8-10 minutes
